**By ThatGVguy**

A fast, no-nonsense guide to the ultimate Resident Evil marathon. We are not just beating the games; we are unlocking the entire franchise like a massive RPG skill tree! :D

## 📜 The Rules of the Challenge (Read This!)
Before we start the timeline, you need to understand how we progress. You will need a RetroAchievements account. If you don't know how to set it up in your emulators (like Dolphin or DuckStation), check out my quick setup guide here: **[RetroAchievements: The Complete Setup Guide](RetroAchievements-Setup-Guide.md)**. If you are already set up, ignore this! :D

**The Terminology:**
* **Beaten vs Mastered:** When you finish a game's story, it's "Beaten". But to progress in this challenge, you must Master the game (100% of the achievements / Platinum). *Note: If this is too hardcore for you, you can play just to beat them, but this guide assumes we are going for 100%!*
* **Req X of Y (Prerequisites):** Some modern games require you to master multiple classic games before you can play them. You need ALL requirements to unlock it!
* **Mastered (Optional):** Side games or HD Steam ports. You can play them whenever you unlock them, but they are not required to progress the main tree.
* **Steam Platforms:** For modern games and Remakes, pick your favorite store (Steam, GOG, etc.) and get all the achievements there.

## 🧟 Raccoon City Outbreak
The timeline splits! You must tackle both to open the heavy doors later.

### Path A: Resident Evil 1 Remake (GC)
**Link:** [Resident Evil 1 Remake (GC)](https://retroachievements.org/game/3358)

**Unlocks:** * RE1 HD on Steam (Mastered Optional).
* **Req 1 of 2** for playing Resident Evil 3 Remake.

### Path B: Resident Evil 3: Nemesis (Classic)
**Pick your platform:**
* [PS1](https://retroachievements.org/game/11265) | [GC](https://retroachievements.org/game/6870) | [DC](https://retroachievements.org/game/9015)

**Unlocks:** * **Req 2 of 2** for Resident Evil 3 Remake *(If you mastered RE1 Remake GC, you can play RE3 Remake now!)*.
* [RE: The Umbrella Chronicles (Wii)](https://retroachievements.org/game/97) *(Mastering this gives you Req 1 of 2 for Darkside Chronicles)*.
* Resident Evil Outbreak File #1.

Escaping the city from different perspectives.

## 🔫 The Modern Era (RE4 & Beyond)
Welcome to the over-the-shoulder revolution.

**Play: Resident Evil 4 (Original)**
**Pick your platform:**
* [GC](https://retroachievements.org/game/2356) | [PS2](https://retroachievements.org/game/2606) | [Wii](https://retroachievements.org/game/186)

**When you MASTER RE4 Original, you unlock:**
* RE4 HD Project (Steam) (Mastered Optional).
* **Req 2 of 2** for Resident Evil 4 Remake *(If you mastered RE2 Remake, you can play it!)*.
* Resident Evil: Revelations 1 (Steam/Modern).

### The Final Run (100% on Steam/Modern Platforms):
From here on out, mastering one game unlocks the next in perfect chronological order:
* Master Revelations 1 -> Unlocks **Resident Evil 5**
* Master Resident Evil 5 -> Unlocks **Resident Evil: Revelations 2**
* Master Revelations 2 -> Unlocks **Resident Evil 6**
* Master Resident Evil 6 -> Unlocks **Resident Evil 7: Biohazard**
* Master Resident Evil 7 -> Unlocks **Resident Evil 8: Village**
* Master Resident Evil 8 -> Unlocks the absolute right to play **Resident Evil 9**
